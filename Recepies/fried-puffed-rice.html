Egg Fry Recepie

A good snack to watch movies at home

Ingredients

  • Puffed Rice
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Sugar
  • A Pan

Procedure

  1. Put the pan to medium heat
  2. Drizzle some oil
  3. Wait for the oil to get heated
  4. Put the egg in
  5. Put few sprinkles of salt on it
  6. Wait till it's cooked
  7. Get it out of the pan
