Pancake Recepie

Pancakes are staple of breakfast for some people. It is filling and can keep you filled for the morning. Below are the ingredients to cook pancakes

Ingredients

  • A pan
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Oil
  • Vanilla Extract

Procedure

  1. Take a bowl
  2. Add one cup of flour
  3. Add one whole egg
  4. Add sugar and salt according to your taste
  5. Add one cup of milk
  6. Add few drops of vanilla extract
  7. Mix them well together
  8. Put the pan in medium heat
  9. Drizzele some oil on it
  10. Put pancakes in batches
  11. Flip when bubbles start forming on top
  12. Take it out the stove when it's golden brown in color with proper texture
