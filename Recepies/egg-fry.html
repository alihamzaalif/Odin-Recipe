Egg Fry Recepie

Eggs are a good source of protein

Ingredients

  • An Egg
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • A Pan

Procedure

  1. Put the pan to medium heat
  2. Drizzle some oil
  3. Wait for the oil to get heated
  4. Put the puff in
  5. Put few sprinkles of salt on it
  6. Put sugar in it
  7. Stirr the sugar and the puffed rice so it covers all the sugar when it's melting
  8. When the melted sugar is covered fully get it out of the pan
  9. Let it cool down
  10. Enjoy
